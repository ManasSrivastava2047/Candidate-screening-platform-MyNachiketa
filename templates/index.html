Connect Google Calendar

Your GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID and GOOGLE_CLIENT_SECRET in .env are already used by the app. This button is a one-time sign-in with your Google account so the app can create Calendar events and Meet links. You are not being asked to enter client ID/secret again.

Scheduling interviews…

Interview Scheduling

Creates a Google Calendar event with a Meet link for each interview candidate. Slots are 30 minutes each, assigned back-to-back. Already-scheduled candidates are skipped.

Leave blank to start at the next weekday 10:00 AM (SCHEDULE_TIMEZONE in .env, default Asia/Kolkata).

Sending interview invites…

Interview Invitation Emails

Sends a templated SMTP email to each scheduled interview candidate with interview date, time, and Google Meet link. Candidates must be scheduled in Step 13 first.
